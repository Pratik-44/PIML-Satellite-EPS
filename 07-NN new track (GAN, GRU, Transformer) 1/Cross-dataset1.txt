GAN (Generative Adversarial Network)
Working of GAN
Two networks:
Generator → creates fake telemetry data
Discriminator → detects real vs fake
They compete → generator improves

Summary:
For this dataset, model performance varies significantly between voltage and power tasks. For voltage, all models perform poorly with negative R², though Transformer is relatively better than GRU, while GAN fails completely. For power, Transformer performs best (R² ~0.81), followed by GRU with moderate performance, whereas GAN again performs very poorly. This indicates that attention-based models generalize better on this dataset, while temporal models (GRU) struggle and GAN remains unsuitable. Overall, Transformer is the most reliable model for this dataset.
